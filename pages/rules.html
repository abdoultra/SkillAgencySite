Rules of the Game

Game Goal

Each player takes on the role of an HR Manager in a digital agency. Your goal is to assemble the perfect team of 3 talents from 5 resumes to win missions The first player who win 3 assignments becomes the best recruiter and wins the game.

Number of players

2 players

Game duration

First 5 minutes for setup

15-20Minutes for rounds

Game Component

25-30 CV cards :¬† each with name, avatar, job, and 3 soft skills 10 Mission cards: show required soft skills, bonuses, penalties, and mission points Game board Game add-on : boost card (QR code available)

How to play

Setup

  1. Shuffle all resume cards and give 5 to each player
  2. Shuffle all mission cards and place the deck in the center
  3. Pick one mission card and place it on the table. This is the mission for round 1.
  4. Each player scans the QR code on the game board to get their Skill Boost. Keep it secret until the reveal phase

Each round has 3 phases
