Rules of the Game

Overview

Skills Agency is a game where each player is an HR Manager. Build the best team of 3 talents from 5 CVs to complete missions. Each mission needs specific skills. Compete in 3 duels, use bonuses and Skill Boosts, and be the first to win 3 missions to become the top HR Manager.

How to play

  1. Choose your 3 talents from the available CV cards.
  2. Read the mission card and select the best suited team.
  3. Resolve duels and apply skill boosts or bonuses.
  4. First player to win 3 missions wins the game.
