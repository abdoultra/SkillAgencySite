THE CARDS

CV CARDS
What they are:
  • CV cards represent the talents you can recruit.
  • Each has:
    • Hard skills
    • Soft skills
    • Special abilities
What they do:

Players recruit CV cards to build a team of talents for missions.

MISSION CARDS
What they are:

Mission cards describe the task your team must complete.

What they do:

They challenge your strategy. Choose wisely which talents fit best.

SKILL BOOST CARDS
What they are:

Special cards used occasionally for scoring or enhancing your team.

What they do:
  • Add bonus points
  • Boost skills
  • Change the course of a round
